Excited to Join Sandia National Laboratories  ·  November 4, 2025

I'm glad to share that I've accepted an offer to join Sandia National Laboratories as a Software Engineering Intern through the TITANS program.

I'll be working on simulating general-purpose program execution on dataflow accelerators — contributing to high-performance computing research using Sandia's Structural Simulation Toolkit (SST). Dataflow architectures execute computation graphs directly rather than flattening them into sequential instructions, and SST lets you model the hardware at a component level to study how programs map onto these designs.

I'm proud of how far I've come to be able to pursue this opportunity, and deeply grateful for my mentors, friends, and family who've supported me along the way. True passion goes a long way.
